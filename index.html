Ryan Gaschel

Student-Micromobility Professional-Cyclist

I currently hold a BA in Environmental Studies from Pacific Lutheran University, and a BS in Homeland Security from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University. I am currently a graduate student in the MS Geospatial Technologies program at the University of Washington. I have over six years of experience as an analyst in the military, with relevant applied experience using GIS tools. I am interested in research around smart cities transportation development and equity programs related to urban transportation.

My graduate capstone project involves constructing an all-in-one micromobility mobile application for the City of Seattle, which provides the user with visibility on the closest form of micromobility available relative to their position, as well as information on the vendor application, their pricing, and equity programs to better inform the urban population of options available to them. The goal of this project is to provide individuals with the best transportation option, and multiplying transportation options available to individuals.
